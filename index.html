About me

I have a passion for the design and development of video games. I love the prototyping process of finding new creative solutions to various problems. I've been able to gather experience as gameplay programmer with the Studio 01 for the games Citywars Tower Defense and Cubio as well as being a quality assurance analyst for the company Huwiz.

I've pushed my knowledge further my creating my own indie game as a solo developer, the silly meta "An Unfinished Game" that I managed to publish on the platform Steam early summer 2025.

Projects

An Unfinished Game

Welcome to the Unfinished Game™ Testing facility! As our new tester, you will have to make your way through a series of unexpected experiments in this first-person adventure. Small puzzles, platforming, meta gameplay and a group of silly NPC awaits you in this unpredictable factory.

My first solo dev project created and published entirely by me over the course of a year on Unity.

Technical aspect:

Unity, C#, Photoshop, Blender, Audacity

Citywars Tower Defense

Citywars TD is a cute multiplayer third person Survival Tower Defense. Build towers, gather resources, craft tools, upgrade weapons, level up buildings, expand your city with your friends and work under pressure before the next incoming wave of monsters.

I've worked at 01 Studio on Citywars Tower Defense/Cubio as gameplay programmer. Created the behaviours of NPCs, defense towers and created plenty of arcade games.

Technical aspect:

Visual scripting using in-house made tools.

Foxy

"Foxy in Peril" is a prototype Android game using AR Foundation and AR Core from Unity. An augmented reality game arena is created by detecting a game map with the smartphone's camera. The goal of the game is to guide Foxy the fox to his den through obstacles.

It's a project that required around twenty hours of work with a team of one artist and two programmers.

Technical aspect:

Unity, Augmented reality, C#, Photoshop, Blender

Fulmina

Fulmina is a prototype Unity game where the player's mission is to cover a procedurally created island with snow. To achieve this, the character will need to cultivate ice, which they can use to fight lava monsters.

Tasks of creating the procedural world, character controller as well as the control of NPC entities.

Technical aspect:

Unity 3D, C#, Photoshop, Blender, Mixamo

Chronospatium

Chronospatium is a prototype 2D procedural sidescroller game created in collaboration between two programmers and two artists. The player's mission is to navigate this randomly generated maze in an attempt to escape in a race against time.

My task was to create the procedural generation as well as character controls.

Technical aspect:

Unity 2D, C#
